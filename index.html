ABOUT US

Sigma Xi, The Scientific Research Honor Society is a non-profit honor society for scientists and engineers which was founded in 1886 at Cornell University by a junior faculty member and a handful of graduate students. Sigma Xi VIT is a student-driven research community at VIT which is established with the vision of empowering students with the knowledge of research in the scientific field. Our community, consisting of a number of like-minded students, has been working to expand the utopian vision of ‘Society Towards Research’.
